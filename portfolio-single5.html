About Roomy

The Roomy APP is designed for users who want to find great roommates and perfect rooms to rent. The APP is offering filter and matching system to help user find their dream roommates who will have fun time with them. It's like a dating APP but who you find is not your sole mate, but the one who will share rent with you.

It's a mockup design for now so I've done everything about the APP, from idea, wireframe, to lofi-hifi prototype, visual design and motion design, I've gain a lot of experience about the whole workflow of APP design, and thanks for professor Suann Fu, she has given a lot of professional opinions to me.

How it looks

Here's some UI looks of Roomy, and I've also built a playable interactive prototype for it to test usability. I've chosen red as a main color to show positive activity and powerful lifestyle, and the whole design taste is modern, clean and very easy to understand, which is very user friendly.

Research & Lo-fi prototype user testing

I've build some paper prototypes and send it to 10 target users to play with, and also I've prepared some core questions to help users send me useful feedback that can help me improve the whole APP. User research is very interesting and super important for me during design process, sometime I can get ideas that I'll never think about. Thanks for all the folks who helped me to finish the part.

Wireframe & Prototype

Wireframe is super important in the design process that it can help me to test the whole APP and test usability. I've build the pure wireframe just with lines and colored cubes at first to find some bugs and I replaced them with lofi UI design to make if clearer.

User Interface Details

This part was finished after I''ve done all the research. And I redesigned the whole interface base on prototype I made before, which is very fast and I've designed all icons to fulfill the theme and style to make everything consistent.
