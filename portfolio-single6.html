About Move UP APP


Product Designer: Wen Wang
Product Manager: Shanshan Zhang

It’s a real project that I designed during my internship in Tiantianld. It’s an App designed to support sport and healthy life style. We designed it base on an original App called Everyday Tennis. Users can use the APP to find other players and place to play tennis, football or other sports, and also, they can split and pay the fee of the place through the app.

Move Up allow users to build social networking and find friends who love same kind of sports and they can play together as a team (which they have built on APP). They also can rate their favorite gym or court and if there're enough people giving a like to a gym, we'll push it to the list and allow users to book the new gym.

How it looks

One of my main roles in the team is UI designer, which means it's my responsibility to offer functional, clean and great looking visual design to the project. I improved the visual system base on the old look and considered about the new feature of the APP, I chose more colors instead of the single green, it's a good way for users to notice it's a totally different APP. And also I keep most of the workflow to make sure our users would not waste much time to familiar with the APP.

Research & Lo-fi prototype user testing

I have deep communicated with Product manager and engineers to talk about how it will be a great user experience and how to realize it in limited time. We improved the wireframe several times and then push them to be visually. I’ve made paper prototype and let our target users, product designers and engineers to play with, get feedback and make improvement base on them to make sure the App is functional and usability.

Wireframe & Prototype

I’ve worked with the Product manager to improve the APP wireframe and UX design. It’s a long-term design and we spend 1 month to improve and test the process base on users’ needs. We started base on the original App and users’ feedback from the research we’ve done before, we moved some secondary function into Left Nav Flyouts, and left 4 main function tabs on the bottom of the main interface. It will support users to follow the gyms they love and if there’re many likes there about a special gym, we’ll contact with the gym and post it on line. We designed more directly visual interface and icon to make it clearer and easier to understand.

Lo-Fi to Hi-Fi Protytype

I've improved the prototype several times to test usability and make sure the workflow is functional and smooth. I did it from wireframe to paper prototype to high quality interactive prototype that allow testing staff to play with it on their phones.

User Interface Details

Details are very important in workflow and final visual design that it can upgrade APP's level and bring better user experience. I've improved the icons, lines, colors and structure and organization a lot from the original one.
