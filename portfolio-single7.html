MyGuide APP Design

Tour Helper Application
3 13

About MyGuide

It’s an App design that can help travellers enjoy a wonderful journey. Users can make special plans base on other’s experience or recommendation by system, and also, they can cloud their plans, and share information with others, and they can find good places nearby when they are travelling.

And also, they can book their hotels, meals and cars through MyGuide and the APP will offer some travel route plans for users to choose, offer them the nearest way to pick their cars from hotel and how will they pass by their favorite spots during their days when travelling.

How it looks

Users can find reference plans on MyGuide easily and make their special routes easily. And also it offers users off-line maps so they can check their route even though there's no signal. The User Interface is much more like a little note book that could be holed in hand or pocket. User can user it to record their days in travelling by taking photos or writing notes.

Icons

Icons are designed base on different function and they are easy to understand. Red and blue give user a fresh and cheer feeling, and it’s suitable for travelling app.

About the IDEA

As an international student, I have to travel a lot during my summer or winter break, and it's also very great to use the part of time to see the world. But sometimes it's very inconvenience to walk around in a total strange country. And also, it happens to others students and people who always travel around to other places. So I combined some core functions and then realize it in my project.

Mockups

I've built some mockups to show how will the APP work and what users can do with it, which is very useful to show clients what could it do and why it's a great idea.
