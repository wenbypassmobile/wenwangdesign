About iScale

TEAM MEMBER: Yi Hao/Tian Wang/Wen Wang

IScale can link with a scale and after an user stand on it, the scale will scan the user’s body and record all data needed, such as weight, height, and fat in different part of body, and then the system will design different plan base on these data, and also, the user can see his information and choose best plan for himself.

It’s a team work that can link people’s life and technology together. The iScale includes a scale device and an App that link with the scale through WiFi. iScale can help people to live a healthier life and help them keep fit.

How it looks

User interface is designed based on iPad device which can be linked with a scale device. I designed all UI part and I’ve chosen simple and texture style. And we have animated all UI to introduce how the app works.

User Interface

One of my main role is User Interface design, and I kept a clean style and fresh feeling for the APP to bring a healthy breath.

Workflow
After users use the scale, they can get details about their bodies, and they also can set suitable exercise plan and recipes base on system’s recommendation. Users can set personal plan on the app and choose different recipes with different sport and time. After users finish their plan, they’ll win a prize medal and they can share it to their social networking.
